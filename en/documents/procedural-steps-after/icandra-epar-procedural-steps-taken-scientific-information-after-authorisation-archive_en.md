Concerning safety, vildagliptin has a hypoglycemia profile superior to that of glimepiride and similar to that of pioglitazone and metformin. Previously, signals concerning hepatic and skin safety have been identified. In the current updated safety data set, slightly higher odds of having a persistent transaminase elevation with vildagliptin were found. Concerning skin safety, the incidences of skin/vascular AEs were low and not statistically significant.   |
