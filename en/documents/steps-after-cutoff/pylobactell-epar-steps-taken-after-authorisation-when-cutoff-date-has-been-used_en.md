## STEPS TAKEN AFTER GRANTING THE MARKETING AUTHORISATION:

## For procedures finalised after 1 October 2001 please refer to module 8B

- Between September 1998 and April 2000, three Type I variations were submitted, seeking to add an additional packaging (assembly) site for the finished product. The latest of these ( I/03) was approved by a Notification to the applicant dated 14 April 2000.
- The Marketing Authorisation Holder applied on 10 October 2000 for a change of address for one of the assembly (packaging) sites for Pylobactell which was also a site where testing takes place.    The  manufacturer  responsible  for  batch  release  in  the  European  Economic  Area remained unchanged.  The change (I/04) was approved by a Notification to the applicant dated 27 October 2000.
- The Marketing Authorisation Holder applied on 27 November 2000 to change the name and address  of  the  Marketing  Authorisation  Holder  for  Pylobactell,  BSIA  Limited,  to  Cambridge Export No. 2 Limited. The change (I/05) was approved by a Notification to the applicant dated 19 December 2000. The revised annexes I, II and III were annexed to this notification, and the revised Commission Decision in all EU languages was issued on 5 March 2001.
- The  Marketing  Authorisation  Holder  applied  on  27  November  2000,  to  delete  a  site  of assembly/batch  release,  site  of  storage/distribution  and  manufacturer  responsible  for  batch release  in  the  EEA,  and  to  add  another  site  of  storage/distribution,  and  as  the  manufacturer responsible for batch release in the EEA. The change (I/06) was approved by a Notification to the applicant dated 19 December 2000. The revised annexes II and IIIB were annexed to this notification, and the revised Commission Decision in all EU languages was issued on 5 March 2001.
- The  Marketing  Authorisation  Holder  applied  on  31  January  2001  to  transfer  the  Marketing Authorisation  for  Pylobactell  to  Torbet  Laboratories,  Ltd.  Limited.  The  EMEA  issued  a Notification  on  the  transfer  (T/07)  on  6  July  2001  The  revised  annexes  I,  II  and  III  were annexed  to  this  notification,  and  the  revised  Commission  Decision  in  all  EU  languages  was issued on 17 September 2001.
