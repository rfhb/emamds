The Patient   | section 4.5 of the SmPC and subsequently, section 2 of the Patient Leaflet in order to inform about drug-drug interaction information with vadadustat and roxadustat, following the outcome of procedure PSUSA/00010296/202411. The results of the in vitro and in vivo studies suggest that bioavailability of vadadustat and roxadustat may be reduced when taken concomitantly with phosphate binders such as Velphoro. Hence, vadadustat and roxadustat should be administered at least one hour before or two hours after Velphoro, to minimise the potential interactions.
