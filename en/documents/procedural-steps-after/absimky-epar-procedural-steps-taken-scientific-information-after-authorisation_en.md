## Variation type IB / EMA/VR/0000275179

SmPC and PL

To update of sections 4.5 and 5.2 of the SmPC in order to add drug-drug interaction information based on results from study CNTO1275CRD1003 and to update sections 4.8 and 5.1 to include patient exposure numbers based on results from study CNTO1275UCO3001. The PL is updated accordingly. The change follows assessment of the same change for the reference product Stelara.
