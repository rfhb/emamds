Also, sub-visible particles were present at levels exceeding the Ph.Eur. limits for parenteralia. However, the product was exempted from this test since it is used with a filter for administration. The new formulation resolves this issue and as a consequence the administration of Erbitux 5 mg/ml does not require use of an in-line filter and this has been adequately reflected in the Summary of Product Characteristics and Package Leaflet.
