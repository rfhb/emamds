## STEPS TAKEN AFTER GRANTING THE MARKETING AUTHORISATION

For procedures finalised after 1 July 2004 please refer to module 8B.

- On 7 May 2001, the European Commission approved a Type I variation for a change in the test procedure of the medicinal product.
- On 7 May 2001, the European Commission approved a Type I variation for a change in the test procedure of the active substance and the medicinal product.
- On 7 May 2001, the European Commission approved a Type I variation for a change in the test procedure of the active substance and the medicinal product.
- On 7 May 2001, the European Commission approved a Type I variation for a change to comply with supplements to pharmacopeias.
- On 16 October 2001, the European Commission approved a Type II variation (II/5) to update the SPC and package leaflet following the assessment of the 1 st PSUR.
- On 8 October 2001, the European Commission approved a Type II variation for an increase in batch size and a change in specification.
